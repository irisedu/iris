Hi! I'm kyo, the maintainer of Iris and all [ksk](https://kyo.seki.pw) services.

I publish to Iris as an extension of my personal knowledge base, which is
powered by [org-mode](https://orgmode.org) and [denote](https://protesilaos.com/emacs/denote).
Iris acts as an important step in my *knowledge pipeline* to transition from
notes which I take only for myself to notes, guides, interactives, etc. which
are public-facing and designed to help others in addition to myself.

This process is part of my endeavor in learning to teach while teaching to
learn. Thanks for visiting Iris!
