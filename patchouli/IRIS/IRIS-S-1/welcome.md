Welcome to Iris, your no-nonsense, open source knowledge base!

## Principles

- **No-nonsense**: Iris will not sell your data, bombard you with popups, or
  serve intrusive ads. All information on Iris is provided for free.
- **Open source**: The source code of Iris and its articles is available on
  [Codeberg](https://codeberg.org/ksk), and all of our site content is available
  under the [Creative Commons BY SA 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)
  license unless otherwise noted.
- **User feedback**: If you have feedback on how Iris can be improved, feel free
  to reach out via email at [contact@seki.pw](mailto:contact@seki.pw).
- **Write about your passion**: If you would like to write about something[^1]
  you are passionate about, reach out via the email above.
- **Quality**: We enforce high quality standards to ensure Iris content is
  accessible and easy to understand.

## Disclaimer

While Iris aims to provide information with the highest accuracy and quality
possible, **we do not claim our articles are absolutely correct or that our
authors have any special qualifications (e.g. graduate-level education or
teaching credentials)**. Additionally, Iris authors have no authority to assess
your knowledge, and as such, we do not award any credentials for completing our
content.

[^1]: Certain content may be deemed unsuitable for Iris.
