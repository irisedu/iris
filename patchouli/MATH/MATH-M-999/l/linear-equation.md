A linear equation is an equation of the form

$$
  a_1x_1 + a_2x_2 + \cdots + a_nx_n = b
$$

for some [real numbers]($r/real-numbers) $$a_1, \ldots, a_n$$.

The more commonly known form $$y = mx + b$$ taught in [algebra]($a/algebra)
is derived from this form.
