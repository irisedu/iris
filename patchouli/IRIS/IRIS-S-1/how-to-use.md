How you decide to use Iris is ultimately up to you. Here are a few suggestions:

## How to use Iris

- **As a companion to your current or previous education**: information on Iris
  can be used to enhance your understanding of current or previous course
  material.
- **To learn concepts for practical usage**: you may want to use information on
  Iris to help complete personal or work-related tasks.
- **To aid in teaching**: if you deem Iris useful enough to teach others,
  [let us know](mailto:contact@seki.pw)!

## How not to use Iris

- Iris is not a replacement for education at a dedicated institution (i.e. a
  school). You will not get a degree from Iris.
- Information on Iris may conflict with what is taught in your course. If this
  is the case, you may want to [contact us](mailto:contact@seki.pw). Follow
  information in your course over what is on Iris.
