Math can be included inline like this: $$\nabla f = \left\langle \frac{\partial
f}{\partial x_1}(x), \ldots, \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_m}(x) \right\rangle$$
and $$y = mx + b$$.

Math can be included in display mode like this:

$$
\begin{equation}
\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L
\end{equation}
$$
